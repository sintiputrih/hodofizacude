Shawn Hatosy Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Shawn Hatosy was born on December 29, 1975 in Frederick, Maryland, U.S.. In 1995, he made his professional acting debut on the television series Homicide: Life on the Street. That same time, he had his first movie appearance in Home for the Holidays. In 1997, he appeared in several films: All Over Me, Inventing the Abbotts, In & Out, The and Postman. He went on to appear as Owen in two episodes of Felicity.

He also appeared in the movies Tangled and John Q. Later, he had roles in the films Swimmers, Alpha Dog, Factory Girl, The Lazarus Project and Public Enemies. On television, his first starring role was as Sammy Bryant on Southland. In 2014, he starred as Terry McCandless on the TV series Reckless. He next appeared as Cpl. Andrew Adams on Fear the Walking Dead. As of 2016, he stars as Pope Cody on Animal Kingdom.
