'Harvey Weinstein Of Bollywood', Says Salman's Ex GF

In a startling move, Bollywood superstar Salman Khan’s ex-girlfriend Somy Ali took to her social media account and dropped a cryptic post. Without taking anybody’s name, she tagged Aishwarya Rai Bachchan as well.

She warned ‘Harvey Weinstein of Bollywood’ that he would be exposed soon. Somy Ali shared a silhouette still from a Bollywood song on her Instagram handle and wrote, “The Harvey Weinstein of Bollywood! You will be exposed. The women you abused will come out one day and share their truth. Just like @aishwaryaraibachchan_arb.”

The still that she shared is from Salman’s old movie Maine Pyar Kiya’s romantic track Aate Jaate Haste Gaate. Without taking anyone’s name, she just tagged Aishwarya, thus leaving many eyebrows raised. India’s first transgender model and actress Nikkiey Chawla left a comment, “Can’t even imagine for a sec what all those girl might hv gone through… that emotional trauma. Kudos to u my darling for raising voice and @aishwaryaraibachchan_arb”.

For the unversed, Somy Ali dated Salman Khan in the 90s for almost a decade. After their breakup, Salman dated Aishwarya in the early 2000s. Aishwarya had even opened up about being in an abusive relationship after the breakup. Meanwhile, Somy Ali starred in many Bollywood films from 1991 to 1998 with leading actors including Suniel Shetty, Saif Ali Khan, Govinda. She currently runs a Non-Profit Organisation in the USA called ‘No More Tears’.
