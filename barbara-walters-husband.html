Barbara Walters Husband: Was Barbara Walters Married?

Barbara Walters Husband: Was Barbara Walters Married?: Barbara Walters, officially known as Barbara Jill Walters was born on September 25, 1929, in Boston, Massachusetts to Lou Walters and Dena Seletsky.

She was an American broadcast journalist and television personality who started her career in the 1960s as a writer and segment producer of women’s interest stories on ‘The Today Show.’

Viewers’ interest in her show resulted in Walters receiving more airtime, and in 1974, she became co-host of the program, the first woman to hold such a title on an American news program.

Barbara Walters was known for her interviewing ability and popularity with viewers. She interviewed every sitting U.S. president and first lady from Richard Nixon to Barack Obama. She also interviewed both Donald Trump and Joe Biden when they were not yet presidents.

Barbara Walters was inducted into the Television Hall of Fame in 1989, and in 2007 received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. She also received a Lifetime Achievement Award from the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences in 2000.

Barbara Walters worked as a journalist from 1951 until her retirement in 2015. She died at her home in Manhattan on Friday, December 30, 2022, at the age of 93.

Barbara Walters Husband: Was Barbara Walters Married?

Barbara Walters was married 4 times to 3 different men. Her first marriage was to Robert Henry Katz, a business executive, and former Navy lieutenant. They married in 1955 and separated in 1957.

Her 2nd husband was Lee Guber, a theatrical producer and theater owner. They married from 1963 to 1976. After their split, Barbara Walters got married to Merv Adelson, the CEO of Lorimar Television.

Barbara and Merv married in 1981 and divorced in 1984. They remarried in 1986 and divorced for the second time in 1992.
