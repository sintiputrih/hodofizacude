240+ Flower Names for Girls (with Meanings)

Flower names for girls are trending in the US and around the world, with flower names such as Iris, Violet, Daisy, Lily, and Rose at the top of international popularity lists.

Along with Lily and Rose, other popular flower names ranking in the US Top 1000 include Briar (for both genders), Dahlia, Holly, Ivy, Magnolia, and Poppy.

In England, the top flower names are Ivy and Lily, while in France it's Rose. In Poland, the most popular flower name for girls is Zuzanna, a former of Susan, which means lily.

What are some unique flower names? Beautiful and rare flower names include Leilani, Flora, Cassia, and Petal. Cute flower names include Daisy, Posy, Tulip, and even Wildflower.

Flower names for baby girls range from the exotic Amaryllis to Zinnia to such everyday flower names as Daisy, Clover, and Marigold. Other plant names for girls include Ivy, Marjoram, and Cassia. There are even a few flower names for baby boys, such as Florian and Peregrine.

Flower baby names can suit every taste and sensibility, from the vintage to the modern, the unique to the familiar. The flower names and meanings here are ordered by their current popularity on Nameberry.
