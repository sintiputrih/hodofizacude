Hyun Bin Biography; Net Worth, Age, Height, Awards, Children, Family, Wife And Movies

Hyun Bin is a South Korean actor who gained recognition in 2005 for his role in a romantic comedy television drama “My name is Kim San-Soon”. Hyun Bin was born on 25 September 1982 in Songpa District, Seoul, in South Korea.

Hyun Bin has an elder brother and he is the youngest of the family. He graduated from Youngdong High School and later studied at Chung-Ang University in 2004 where he majored in Theater Studies. Hyun Bin returned t Chung-Ang University to obtain a master’s degree in 2009.

Hyun Bin rose to prominence after starring in the romantic comedy television drama My Name Is Kim Sam-soon in 2005. Since then, he has starred in a number of other famous television shows, including; Secret Garden (2010–2011), Memories of the Alhambra (2018–2019), Crash Landing on You (2019–2020).

Hyun Bin’s fame grew even more after starring in a string of box office hits, including the action-thriller Confidential Assignment (2017), the crime thrillers The Swindlers (2017), and The Negotiation (2018), and the zombie horror Rampant (2018).

Hyun Bin has been honored for several awards during his career, including five Baeksang Arts Awards, and has received numerous acting accolades, including the Grand Prize (Daesang) for TV at the 47th Baeksang Arts Awards.
