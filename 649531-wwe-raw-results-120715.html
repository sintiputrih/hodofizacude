May 8, 2024 Aldo Pusey

WWE RAW Results (12/7): Roman Reigns Goes Face to Face with Sheamus, ECW Legend Returns, 16 Man Tag

Owens misses a running senton. Ziggler punches Owens a few times, but he misses his corner splash attempt. Owens grabs Ziggler and almost throws Ziggler through the ring post. Ziggler is almost counted out. As soon as Ziggler gets in the ring Owens finally hits the running senton for a long two count.

After a short break Ziggler moves as Owens charges into the turnbuckle. Ziggler hits a huge clothesline, followed by a leaping elbow drop. Ziggler goes for his leaping DDT once again, but Owens catches him and tosses him over the top rope. Owens goes outside and gets distracted long enough for Ziggler to finally hit his leaping DDT. Ziggler rolls Owens back in the ring and pins him, but Owens kicks out at 2.5. Ziggler goes for a famasser, but Owens reverses it into a German suplex.

Owens misses the cannonball in the corner and Ziggler hits the famasser for another two count. Ziggler misses the zig zag, Owens hits a superkick, Ziggler falls into the ropes and superkicks Owens. Owens still manages to kick out. Ziggler hits the ropes and runs right into a pop up power bomb for the win.

Winner- Kevin Owens

After the match Dean Ambrose comes out to the ring eating popcorn and drinking a soda. Owens gets in Ambrose’s face. Ambrose tosses the popcorn and the soda in Owen’s face and walks away. Owens is livid.
