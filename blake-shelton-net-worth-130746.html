Blake Shelton Net Worth 2024

Blake Shelton is an American country music singer and songwriter who was born on June 18, 1976 in Ada, OK. He is best known for his single "Austin" which topped the Country Billboard for five weeks in 2001 and his first three albums were all Gold-certified. In 2014, he was awarded Male Vocalist of the Year at the CMA Awards.

About

Country star whose single "Austin" rose to #1 on the County Billboard for five weeks in 2001. His first three albums were Gold-certified. At the 2014 CMA Awards, he was named Male Vocalist of the Year.

Before Fame

He began singing and playing guitar when he was in middle school and wrote his first song at age fifteen. He received a Denbo Diamond Award in Oklahoma when he was sixteen.

Trivia

His brother Richie died in 1990, and the song he wrote about him, "Over You," won the CMA Song of the Year Award in 2012.  He was voted sexiest man alive by People magazine in 2017. 

Family Life

He grew up with his brother Richie and his sister Endy. He married Kaynette Williams on November 17, 2003; after their divorce in 2006, he married Miranda Lambert on May 14, 2011. He and Miranda divorced in 2015, then he began dating Gwen Stefani.

Associated With

In 2011, he began working as a vocal coach on the NBC show The Voice, alongside Christina Aguilera.
