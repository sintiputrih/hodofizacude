Is Golfer Chad Ramey Married? Everything On His Wife And Family Details

PGA golfer Chad Ramey, age 29, is engaged to his soon-to-be wife Kelly Nolan and they are set to wed on December 4, 2021.

Chad Ramey Age & Wikipedia

A native of Tupelo, Mississippi, Chad Ramey, age 29, was born on 3rd August 1992.

He is a graduate of Itawamba Agricultural High School and was a Mississippi University fan while growing up. The golfer has marked his golf ball with an Icelandic coin since college.

One of his earliest and fondest golf memory is attending The Masters at age 10.

Besides golf, he loves watching television shows like Blacklist, Blindspot, The Voice, American Horror Story, and Lost.

Who Are Chad Ramey Parents?

Chad Ramey’s parents were very supportive of his career and are his biggest strength to date.

Ramey got interested in golf because of his father, who was a golf course superintendent. He used to accompany his father, Stanley at games and eventually was hooked into the sport.

His mother goes by the name Trish and he also has two sisters.
