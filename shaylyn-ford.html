Wiki/Biography

Shaylyn Ford was born in 1984 (age 38 years; as of 2022), in Granville, Ohio, US. She attended the University of Findlay, Ohio, US, from 2003 to 2004. In 2006, she did a licensing course in cosmetology from Summit Salon Academy, Perrysburg, Ohio, US.

Career

In May 2007, she started working as a cosmetologist at a salon in Maumee, Ohio, US, and worked there for almost three years. In March 2007, she started on-site hair and makeup services for weddings, photoshoots, and other events (Shaylynford.com). In 2018, she joined the salon Venus and Mars Lifestyle Collective, Columbus, Ohio, as a salon director. She has been associated with the makeup artists group ‘Team Flawless First.’ In 2021, she participated in the Mrs. Ohio America beauty pageant and won the title on July 30, 2021.

Shaylyn Ford being crowned as Mrs Ohio America

She then represented Ohio city in the beauty pageant Mrs. America, on November 27, 2021, and she won the title. She went on to represent America in the Mrs. World beauty pageant, and on January 15, 2022, she was crowned as Mrs. World 2021 title. The event was held at Westgate Las Vegas Resort & Casino, Las Vegas, Nevada, USA. In the competition, she was asked about her plans for the pageants ‘Beauty For Good’ and ‘Victoria’s Voice charities.’ She said,

I’ve already started. My first meeting is on Monday with the Beauty For Good team and I’m so excited to get some victory clubs going in our state. I’ve worked with a lot of our state queens and we want to continue expanding the programme. I am so excited to be partnering with [drug charity] Victoria’s Voice and Beauty For Good, and I think we can make a real change. I have had opioid addiction hit my own family. I’ve lost people who I love and I’m very passionate about taking this forward. Together I feel we can take massive ground and I can’t wait to do it.”
