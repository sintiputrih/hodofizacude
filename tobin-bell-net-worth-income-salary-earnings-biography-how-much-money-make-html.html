Tobin Bell (born Joseph Henry Tobin, Jr.; August 7, 1942) is an American film and television character actor, noted for his role as John Kramer/Jigsaw of the Saw film series. After years of work performing stand-ins and background work on films, he received his first big acting role in Mississippi Burning (1988) and went on to appear in made-for-television films and guest star in television programmes during the 1990s. A life member of The Actors Studio, Bell also voice acted his Jigsaw character in two video games based on the movie, Saw and its sequel, Saw II: Flesh & Blood. Bell’s depiction of Jigsaw has garnered him five award nominations and two wins. Before his breakthrough with Saw, Bell appeared in many notable films including Tootsie (1982), The Verdict (1982), Mississippi Burning (1988), Loose Cannons (1990), Goodfellas (1990), Ruby (1992), The Firm (1993), In the Line of Fire (1993), Malice (1993), The Quick and the Dead (1995), Brown’s Requiem (1998), a voice role in The Road to El Dorado (2000), and a narration in Boiling Point (1993).
