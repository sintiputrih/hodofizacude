Rachel McAdams Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Rachel McAdams (born in London, Ontario, Canada on November 17, 1978) is a Canadian actress. She made her acting debut in 2001 in the Italian-Canadian film, My Name is Tanino. Rachel got her first big break in the comedy, The Hot Chick, but really came into fame playing Regina George in the classic film, Mean Girls. From there, Rachel starred in other gigantic hits, like The Notebook, Wedding Crashers, and Red Eye. After taking a relative break, Rachel came back to acting in 2009, starring in movies like Midnight in Paris and The Vow.

Best Known For:
Rachel McAdams is best known for being a prominent actress, starring in movies like Mean Girls and The Notebook.

Personal Life:
Rachel McAdams grew up in London, Canada near Toronto. She attended college at the Central Elgin Collegiate Center. Rachel dated Ryan Gosling in the two years following The Notebook — they split in 2007. After meeting Michael Sheen while filming Midnight in Paris in 2010, the two began dating but split after two years.
