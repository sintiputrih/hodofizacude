Ryan Reynolds Trolls Wife Blake Lively with Candid Photos on Her Birthday

  • Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively regularly troll one another on social media.
  • In celebration of Lively's 32nd birthday, Reynolds shared a selection of rare photos of the actress, most of which show her with her eyes closed.
  • Just last week, Reynolds shared a seriously adorable Instagram post in which he revealed the best present he's ever received from his wife.

Blake Lively just turned 32, and her husband was ready to celebrate. As usual, Ryan Reynolds was prepared for his wife's birthday, this time deciding to share a series of never-before-seen photos on Instagram for the occasion.

Related Story

Though many might expect adorable snaps of the happy couple, true fans of Lively and Reynolds will be well aware of their incessant online trolling. And Reynolds really went to town for Lively's 32nd birthday.

With the simple caption, "Happy birthday, @blakelively," Reynolds shared selfies taken with his wife—with a twist. In each of the snaps, the Gossip Girl actress is either blinking or blurry (though, that still can't detract from how beautiful she is).

Despite some of Reynolds's photos being low-key hilarious, it simply wouldn't be a birthday celebration without a little light trolling on social media.

For instance, in 2017, Lively celebrated her husband's birthday by sharing a pic of Ryan Gosling on Instagram. Reynolds was barely visible in the shot, as he had been masterfully cropped out by his wife.

The actress announced in May 2019 that she is currently pregnant with her third child with Reynolds.
