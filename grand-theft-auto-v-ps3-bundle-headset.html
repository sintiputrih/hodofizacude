Grand Theft Auto V Gets A PS3 Bundle, Exclusive Headset And New Screens

Recommended Videos

Sony has announced that an exclusive  Grand Theft Auto V PS3 bundle will be made available once the game launches on September 17th. For $299 you will receive a 500GB PlayStation 3, a copy of GTA V and a 30-day PlayStation Plus trial membership.

Additionally, Rockstar Games has collaborated with Sony in order to create a custom designed Grand Theft Auto V Pulse – Elite Edition headset that features a style designed specifically for GTA V. In case you’re unable to infer what the design was inspired by, it’s money. The new custom Pulse headset will be available alongside the game’s launch exclusively at GameStop for $179.99

Sony is aiming to deliver a specifically created audio experience for GTA V that is exclusive for the PlayStation 3 platform. The Pulse – Elite Edition headset will have an exclusive Grand Theft Auto V audio mode that takes full advantage of the BassImpact technology embedded in the headset. The goal is to provide the ultimate Grand Theft Auto V experience and it starts by providing the exclusive audio mode for free from the Pulse – Elite Manager App. The exclusive GTAV audio mode will enhance the game’s most epic moments by amplifying the effects of events like car crashes and explosion.

Additionally, the crew over at Rockstar has updated their GTA V splash page with nine brand new images pulled straight from the game. The new batch of screens doesn’t really reveal any new information but it certainly gives you something to drool over until the game launches on September 17. You can check them out below.
