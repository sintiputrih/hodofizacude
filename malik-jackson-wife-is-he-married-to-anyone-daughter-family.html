The former Pro Bowl defensive tackle announced his retirement on Friday, July 14th. He has been playing in the NFL for the last 10 seasons.

Malik Barron Jackson is an American former football defensive tackle who played in the NFL. He was born on January 11, 1990, in Northridge, Los Angeles.

Jackson’s football journey began at Birmingham High School, where he played high school football. After graduating high school, he played college football at Tennessee.

The defensive tackle was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the fifth round of the 2012 NFL Draft. Since being selected, he recorded 292 tackles and 35.5 sacks in 142 career games.

In 2015, he helped the Broncos to a victory in Super Bowl 50. Further, he also earned Pro Bowl honors in 2017 after registering a career-high eight sacks.
