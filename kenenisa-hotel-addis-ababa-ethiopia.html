Kenenisa Hotel, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Located in the heart of Bekoji, Kenenisa Hotel is a hotel with free wifi, non-smoking rooms, bar and a 5.0 guest reviews rating.

Kenenisa Hotel has one of the most exquisite menus in the city, the on site restaurant treats guests to really fine dining. Guests can also unwind at the bar and enjoy an exotic drink.

Kenenisa Hotel has family friendly rooms that are well-spaced and are centrally air conditioned. High speed complimentary Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV, mini bar, tea & coffee making facilities.
