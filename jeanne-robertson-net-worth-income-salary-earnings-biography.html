Jeanne Robertson Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Jeanne Robertson, also known as Jeanne Swanner Robertson, was born on September 21, 1943, in New York City, and is a nationally renowned comedian and professional speaker. She was crowned Miss North Carolina in 1963, when she was only 19 years old. Robertson cites her reign as the impetus for her professional success. It is possible to hear her stories on XM Radio’s Channel 98, “Laugh USA,” as well as Sirius Radio’s Blue Collar Comedy channel 103 (XM 97), “Laugh Break channel 105,” and other programmes on a daily basis. She draws inspiration for her comedic material from real-life events. On April 29, 2008, she delivered a speech at the White House in celebration of National Volunteer Week, which was held to recognise the 1,300 volunteers who give their time to serve at the White House.
