Lil Durk Teaming Up With Morgan Wallen| Insights About His Net Worth, Girlfriend, &amp; Married Life

Lil Durk surprised his fans by collaborating with the country music singer Morgan Wallen and released a new song, Broadway Girls on 17 December. It was interesting for a rapper to collaborate with a country music singer and to add up, Morgan Wallen was involved in a major racial controversy earlier this year. However, the singer had apologized for his inappropriate behaviors and made several donations to various organizations including BMAC.

Their new song has gained many attention due to the singer’s past issues and many fans have shown support for them but some of them are not satisfied with the rapper’s choice to collaborate with the singer who used the N-word. Morgan was seen screaming the N-word with a friend outside Nashville’s house in a video clip that made a headline at the end of January this year.

After that, he was considered ineligible for the April 2021 ACM Awards and the singer apologized for his actions and spent months out of the public eye. The singer admitted his wrongdoings and shared that he spent time with members od the Black Music Action Coalition and more similar institutions to learn how his actions were wrong and affected the black communities.

Net Worth Of Lil Durk

The net worth of the American rapper, Lil Durk, is $3 million who is also the founder of the record label Only the Family.

The rapper also launched OTF Gaming in April 2021 after pandemic as he shared, “Pre-pandemic I don’t think I had the chance to play a video game in the last 15 years. I had no interest, and definitely didn’t have the time, but sitting at home every day brought about a new way for one to entertain themselves.” He shared with Afrotech, “This led to not only our team being involved in recreational gaming, but also researching into the streaming space and connecting the dots back to OTF as a brand.”

The rapper is mostly known for his dark, violent and nihilistic subgenre of trap and rap music. Lil Durk became popular form his mixtape, Signed to the Streets released in 2013 which was his second act. It was named the 8th Best Mixtape of the Year by Rolling Stones that year. He released his debut studio album Remember My Name in 2015 after signing with Def Jam Records. The album reached #2 on the US rap and US R&B/Hip-Hop charts. His popular singles are  Just Cause Ya’ll Waited 2, 3 Headed Goat, Backdoor, The Voice, My Beyonce and more. He has collaborated with many famous artists like Meek Mill, French Montana, Travis Scott, Will.i.am, Young Thug, RiFF RaFF and more.

Lil Durk Dating Life

The real name of the rapper, Lil Durk is Durk Derrick Banks born in 1992 in Englewood, Chicago. He had dated Nicole Covone from 2008 to 2013. The couple first met in 2008 and went onto date and got married for three years. They also welcomed two children together, Angelo Banks and Bella Banks in 2011 and 2013 respectively. The pair got divorced when his third child was born with different woman.

The rapper then got involved romantically with rapstress Dej Loaf in 2014. They also collaborated on the song, My Beyonce in 2015 and were seen kissing on stage. The two reportedly broke up by the end of 2016 and Loaf had confirmed their break up and shared that they were still friends.

Lil Durk then dated Takala Welch in 2016 for a short period of time. He was also secretly dating Mia Burks as revealed by her former friend.

However, the rapper is presently dating Instagram model, India Royale since 2017. They welcomed two child together, both daughter, Willow and Skyler but the couple faced many cheating allegations. Rumors were surfaced about their split but the rapper had to deny it again and again. Lil Durk has always been responding to the rumors of their break up by posting some loved up photos of the couple. He also claimed and confirmed they are still together writing, “It p*ss me off how y’all change sh*t around tryna make it like somebody a h*e talking about her like talking bout my kids kill that rumor my b*tch ain’t nun like you goofy h*es.”
