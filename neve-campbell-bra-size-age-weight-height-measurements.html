Neve Campbell Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Neve Campbell was born on October 3, 1973 in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. She trained at the National Ballet School of Canada. Dance injuries, however, put a halt to her dancing career.

In 1992, she had her first starring role in the Canadian youth television series Catwalk. She rose to fame in 1994 after being cast as Julia Salinger in the drama series Party of Five.

She further gained worldwide fame when she won the leading role in Wes Craven’s Scream in 1996. The film was a commercial hit and was also critically acclaimed, earning her the Saturn Award for Best Actress.
