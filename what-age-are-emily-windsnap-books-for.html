What kind of book is Emily Windsnap?

children’s fantasy novels Emily Windsnap is a series of children’s fantasy novels written by British author Liz Kessler, inaugurated by The Tail of Emily Windsnap in 2003 and continuing as of 2020. It is illustrated primarily by Sarah Gibb and published by Orion Children’s Books in Britain, and Candlewick Press in America.
