Eevee on the Ball (Pokmon Futsal 2)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Eevee on the Ball  
   
For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Eevee.

Eevee on the Ball is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Pokémon Futsal collection.

Card text

Make the Assist
Attach a basic Energy card from your hand to 1 of your Benched Pokémon.
Tackle20

Pokédex data

Release information

This card was the first Pokémon Futsal promotional card given away to customers who purchased at least £15 worth of eligible Pokémon Trading Card Game products at participating GAME stores in England or with the purchase of bundles including the card on the GAME website. Unlike the in-store promotion, the online bundles were open to all countries in the United Kingdom. This card was available from September 11, 2020 for as long as stocks lasted. It also features the Pokémon Futsal logo in the bottom right corner of the artwork.

Trivia

This card was banned from the 2022 World Championships due to not being distributed worldwide.

Origin

Tackle is a move in the Pokémon games that Eevee can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword.
