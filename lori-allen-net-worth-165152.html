Lori Allen Net Worth

About

Television host of Say Yes To The Dress: Atlanta and its spin-off Say Yes To The Dress: Bridesmaids. She owns Bridals by Lori, Atlanta's largest bridal boutique.

Before Fame

She opened her first store in Atlanta on December 27, 1980, twelve days after her graduation from Columbia College in South Carolina.

Trivia

Her struggle with breast cancer and subsequent lumpectomy and double mastectomy in 2012 have made her an active breast cancer awareness advocate. She worked with TLC to make a special television episode titled Say Yes To The Cure: Lori's Fight.

Family Life

She married her college sweetheart Eddie in 1979 during her senior year of college. She has a daughter named Mollie and a son named Cory.

Associated With

She was photographed with her co-host Monte Durham and Cake Boss Buddy Valastro at TLC's Summer Block Party in 2015.
