Marly Van Der Velden Net Worth

Marly Van Der Velden is a Dutch actress and entrepreneur born on January 6, 1988 in Delft, Netherlands. She is best known for her role as Nina Sanders Mauricius on the long-running Dutch soap opera Goede Tijden, Slechte Tijden since 2005. In addition to her acting career, she is also a successful fashion and jewelry designer, having founded the companies Label 13 and Kuise Meisjes.
