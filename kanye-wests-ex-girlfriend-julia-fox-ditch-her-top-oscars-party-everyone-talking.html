Kanye Wests Ex-Girlfriend Julia Foxs Decision To Ditch Her Top At An Oscars Party Has Everyone

Highlights

  • Julia Fox's style is bold and daring, often attracting attention for her risqué outfit choices.
  • Dating Kanye West influenced her fashion evolution, with a shift towards more provocative looks.
  • Her relationship with West was brief but received significant public attention, leading to a fast breakup.

Kanye West's ex-girlfriend, Julia Fox, is a celebrity in her own right, but her style has also been the topic of much discussion. She has had some questionable looks over the years, and the "Uncut Gems" star has proven she's willing to take a risk and not afraid to show some skin. This was the case with her latest look, which she wore to the 32nd annual Elton John AIDS Foundation Academy Awards Oscar viewing party on March 10, 2024.

Fox was photographed at the star-studded party wearing a black dress from DSquared2, which revealed much of the top half of her body. The mini-dress had nipple tassels and gold chains, attracting a lot of attention, and the look was referred to as "NSFW." But is this precisely what Fox wants?

What Has Julia Fox Said About Her Style?

When Julia Fox started dating Kanye West in January 2022, her approach to fashion underwent a notable change. While her relationship with the rapper was short-lived, her looks continued to get her attention. She's found her on several lists detailing her outfit choices, from daring to inspiring, including Vogue's "Everything Julia Fox teaches us about dressing for the female gaze." Whether you love or hate her style, Fox has worked hard to create a narrative around her outfits.

What Happened Between Julia Fox And Kanye West?

Fox and West were a surprising couple, and their romance was very public when they first got together in early 2022. This gave fans a front-row seat to their coupling with photos posted on Instagram and interviews. "I mean, I'm still in shock," Fox told Interview in January 2022. "Ye had an entire hotel suite full of clothes. It was every girl's dream come true. It felt like a real Cinderella moment," she said at the time. "I don't know how he did it or how he got all of it there in time. But I was so surprised." She was also happy to go along with his plans.
