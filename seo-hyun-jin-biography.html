Seo Hyun-jin Biography

QUICK FACTS
Nationality South Korea
Born February 27, 1985 (Seoul)

BIOGRAPHY
South Korean actress and singer Seo Hyun-jin debuted as the lead vocalist of the South Korean girl group MILK in 2001, with whom she continued until the group disbanded in 2003. She released solo songs after the group’s dissolution before transitioning to performing in 2006 with his first musical, The Sound of Music . She made appearances in several television series and films. She eventually gained wider recognition for her starring role as Oh Hae-young in the romantic comedy television series Another Miss Oh (2016), which was followed by starring roles in the medical melodrama Dr. Romantic (2016–2017) and romantic dramas Temperature of Love (2017) and Inner Beauty (2018). In 2022, Seo stars in the SBS drama Why Her , which follows the story of callous lawyer Oh Soo-jae and law student Gong Chan (Hwang In-youp) as they fall in love with her.
