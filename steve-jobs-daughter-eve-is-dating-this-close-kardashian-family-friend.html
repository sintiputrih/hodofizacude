Steve Jobs Daughter Eve Is Dating This Close Kardashian Family Friend

It seems that Steve Jobs' 22-year-old daughter has found love with a close friend of the Kardashian/Jenner clan. Eve Jobs, the youngest daughter of the Apple founder, took to Instagram to seemingly announce that she is now dating singer/songwriter Harry Hudson.

Jobs posted a series of photos on her Instagram that show the two snuggling together along the California coast, while another photo shows them embracing in a kiss. Jobs captioned her four-photo collage with "team" along with a black heart. Hudson responded in a comment, "Forever as f---."

Hudson also showed love to his new girlfriend with his own series of snaps. The 27-year-old shared photos of the pair in matching reindeer onesies, as well as a photo that shows Jobs and Hudson in matching jackets with the backs reading, "Hey, I'm here for you."

Several celebrities gave their support to the new A-list couple, including Hudson's close pal, Khloe Kardashian, who commented with a series of purple heart emojis on his photos.

Fai Khadra, who has also been hanging out with the Kardashian and Jenner women for quite some time, even fueling romance rumors with Kendall Jenner at one point, gave his stamp of approval with three smiling faces with heart-eyes emojis.

Hudson isn't just known as one of the Kardashian's best friends, of course. He has released two albums, including, Yesterday's Tomorrow Night. His latest record, Hey, I'm Here For You, was released in 2020.

Jobs, meanwhile, is a distinguished equestrian and usually posts photos of her competitions on her Instagram. It also looks like she's taking up a modeling career, too. Together, the two make quite an accomplished pair.
