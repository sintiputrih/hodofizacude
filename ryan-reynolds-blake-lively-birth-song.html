Ryan Reynolds Played Marvin Gaye While Blake Lively Gave Birth

Blake Lively is a mother to two beautiful children, but giving birth to them was a bit unconventional. According to the actress, her husband Ryan Reynolds played "Let's Get It On" by Marvin Gaye while she was in labor. (It's not quite the birth anthem we were expecting, but hey, at least it's soothing. And would you expect anything different from Reynolds?)

"My doctor was laughing so hard that I thought she was gonna drop our baby," Lively told designer Michael Kors in a new video. She revealed the surprising anecdote to him during a game of Fact or Fiction.

Lively uncovered plenty of gems during her game with Kors: her first kiss was on camera when she was 16, her first dog was named 405 after the freeway and her childhood celebrity crush was David Letterman.
