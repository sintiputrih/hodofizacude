Lindsay Lohan Poses in Pink Underwear for Halloween

Did Lindsay Lohan run out of Halloween costume ideas? The star decided to strip down to just her underwear on Sunday, October 30, to wish her fans a happy Halloween.

Posing in nothing more than a lacy pink bra and panties, Lohan took a mirror selfie to post to her Instagram account to celebrate the spooky holiday.

“#halloweencostume #halloweenisaworkout#halloween #everydaywewearpink and no filter,” she wrote next to the racy photograph.

Her snap was met with some confusion from her followers who asked what the costume was supposed to be. Others slammed her for using Halloween as an excuse to be “slutty,” while some thought she looked “hot.”

Lohan didn’t hit the town in her barely there outfit though, and was actually headed to bed.

Shortly after sharing the selfie she posted a black-and-white photo with her friends and captioned it: “Sorry I couldn’t spend tonight with my beautiful friends. Off to bed before early flight to turkey.”

The 30-year-old has been spending time in the country, both vacationing and visiting refugees in a camp near the country’s border with Syria.

She recently opened up about moving away from Hollywood to London in 2012, admitting it was the best thing she ever did.

The actress spoke during a during a rare interview on Turkey’s Habertürk TV on Wednesday, October 12, and said she enjoyed being away from the spotlight.

"In London we have news and Bloomberg all day," she revealed. "I don't have, like, Access Hollywood and all these other shows. I don't see this anymore. For four years now, I don't see this, and I love it. … This is great for me!"
