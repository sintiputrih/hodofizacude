Honkai Star Rail: Jarilo-VI Treasure Chests Locations

Here are the locations of all the chests in Jarilo-VI in Honkai: Star Rail that you can open to get free rewards.

First Published April 26, 2023, 15:43

Just like Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail has treasure chests spread across all areas that you can find out by exploring, solving puzzles, completing quests, and defeating enemies. By opening them, you will be able to gain rewards like Stellar Jades, Credits, EXP, and more, and while some players like to find these on their own, others might not want to spend so much time in it.

Honkai: Star Rail has three major locations, including Herta Space Station, Jarilo-VI, and Xianzhou Luofu, which are divided into different zones. In this guide, we have mentioned all the locations of chests in Jarilo-VI in Honkai: Star Rail to save you some time. You can have a look at all the chest locations in Herta Space Station in our dedicated guide.
