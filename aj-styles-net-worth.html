AJ Styles Net Worth

AJ Styles is a professional wrestler from the United States with a net worth of $6 million.

He began his career on the independent circuit and later worked briefly for WCW and WWE. Styles also wrestled in Ring of Honor, but he is best remembered for his tenure in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) from 2002 to 2013.

Styles won the NWA World Heavyweight Championship three times, the NWA World Tag Team Championship four times, the TNA World Heavyweight Championship twice, the TNA World Tag Team Championship twice, and the TNA X Division six times.

From 2014 to 2016, he wrestled in ROH, New Japan Pro-Wrestling, and on the independent circuit before signing with WWE. He was the WWE Champion in his first year.

AJ Styles has been regarded as a pillar of the wrestling industry for over two decades, revered by fans and colleagues alike, and is regarded as one of the world’s greatest professional wrestlers.

AJ Styles was inducted into the Wrestling Observer Newsletter Hall of Fame in 2017 after winning 10 Wrestling Observer Newsletter awards, including Wrestler of the Year in 2015 and 2016.
