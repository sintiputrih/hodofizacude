Lourdes Leon's daring cut-out catsuit will make your jaw drop

Madonna's daughter turned heads at the H&M x Mugler launch

    Lourdes Leon certainly takes after her famous mom, Madonna, when it comes to making a red carpet statement. 

    The 26-year-old stole the show at the H&M x Mugler launch in New York City on Wednesday, wearing a daring, black cut-out catsuit from the new collaboration. 

    Lourdes commanded attention in the striking ensemble, which exposed much of her skin including her toned, dancer's legs, tiny waist, and cleavage. 

    She toned down the look with a floor-length, fluffy black coat that fell off one shoulder, and added a pair of strappy black heels.

    MORE: Lourdes Leon channels mom Madonna in lace-up corset

    Her raven locks were worn down and straight with a center parting, while her makeup accentuated her plump pout with overlined lips and fluttery eyelashes. 

    Lourdes wasn't the only star to attend the glitzy event as Pamela Anderson also made an entrance in an eye-catching see-through catsuit. 

    The H&M x Mugler collection will give fashion fans the chance to own one of the late designer Thierry Mugler's signature looks at a fraction of their original price. 

    "Each piece is defined by the irrepressible Mugler energy: playful, youthful, body-positive and complimenting each individual's identity," according to H&M's website. 

    Like her mom, Lourdes is a trained dancer and has been making a name for herself lately as a sought-after model. She also released her debut EP, , under the moniker Lolahol in November.

     Lourdes, affectionately known as Lola, is the oldest of Madonna's six children, and the pair have an incredibly close bond.

    Madonna shares Lourdes with ex-boyfriend Carlos Leon and also has son Rocco with ex-husband Guy Ritchie. The star went on to adopt children David, Mercy, and twins Esther and Stella from Malawi. 

    While the singer is quite private about her personal life, she previously opened up about her relationship with her children and revealed her pride for Lola. 

    Talking to , she said her daughter was "insanely talented". She said: "I'm green with envy because she's incredible at everything she does – she's an incredible dancer, she's a great actress, she plays the piano beautifully, she's way better than me in the talent department."
