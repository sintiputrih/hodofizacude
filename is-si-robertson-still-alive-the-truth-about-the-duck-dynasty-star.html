Is Si Robertson Still Alive? The Truth About the Duck Dynasty Star

Si Robertson, also known as Uncle Si, is an American television personality, veteran, and a retired reed maker for duck calls at Duck Commander. He is best known for his role on A&E’s Duck Dynasty, on which he has emerged as a breakout cast member. He has also appeared on other shows such as the Outdoor Channel’s Buck Commander and The Duckmen of Louisiana, and was the eponymous host of the Duck Dynasty spin-off Going Si-Ral.

But is Si Robertson still alive? And what is he doing now? Here are some facts you need to know about the beloved Uncle Si.

Si Robertson’s Early Life and Military Career

Si Robertson was born on April 27, 1948, in Vivian, Louisiana, the sixth of seven children. He grew up in a poor family with few amenities. He attended North Caddo High School in Vivian, where he played football wearing the same #10 jersey as his older brother Phil. He dropped out of Louisiana Tech University after three quarters and was drafted into the United States Army during the Vietnam War. He served for 25 years and retired in 1993 with the rank of Sergeant First Class. During his military service, he received several awards and decorations, including the Army Commendation Medal. According to Wikipedia.

Si Robertson’s Business with Duck Commander

After retiring from the Army in 1993, Si Robertson joined his brother Phil’s Duck Commander business, which makes duck calls and other hunting products. Si’s job is to fashion the reeds that are inserted in every patented duck call. He is also known for his humorous stories and antics on the show Duck Dynasty, which follows the lives of the Robertson family and their business. Si is rarely seen without one of his plastic cups drinking iced tea, which he treats with equal importance. He also carries a Tupperware container with beans and rice that his mother sent him during the Vietnam War. According to Wikipedia.

Si Robertson’s Personal Life and Family

Si Robertson married Christine Raney in 1971 and they have two children, Trasa and Scott. They also have eight grandchildren. Si is a devout Christian and often shares his faith on the show. He is also an avid hunter and fisherman and enjoys playing golf. He has written two books: Si-cology 1: Tales and Wisdom from Duck Dynasty’s Favorite Uncle and Si-renity: How I Stay Calm and Keep the Faith. According to Married Celeb.

Si Robertson’s Death Hoax

In March 2020, a website called Conservative Tears published an article claiming that Si Robertson had been found dead in the woods near the Duck Dynasty warehouse. The article was a hoax and intended to be satirical, as stated by the website’s disclaimer. However, some people believed it was true and shared it on social media. Si Robertson himself debunked the rumor on his Facebook page, saying it was only 95% true and that he was still alive and kicking. According to Snopes.com.

Si Robertson’s Current Status

Si Robertson is still alive and well as of May 2023. He is still working at Duck Commander and appearing on various TV shows. He also hosts a podcast called The Si Robertson Show, where he talks about his life, faith, family, and hunting adventures. He has also launched his own line of iced tea called Uncle Si’s Iced Tea, which comes in four flavors: sweet, unsweetened, peach, and half & half. He is also active on social media, where he posts updates and photos of his family and friends. According to Military Wiki.

Conclusion

Si Robertson is one of the most popular and beloved stars of Duck Dynasty. He is known for his funny stories, quirky habits, and strong faith. He has had a long and interesting life, from serving in the Army to making duck calls to starring on TV shows. He is still alive and enjoying his life with his wife, children, grandchildren, and fans. He is an inspiration to many people who admire his humor, wisdom, and positivity.
