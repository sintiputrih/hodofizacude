Randy Orton to Co-Star in New Movie Alongside Former Raw Guest Host

According to Deadline.com, WWE Smackdown Live star Randy Orton will be starring in the upcoming movie “Changeland”, alongside former WWE Raw guest host Seth Green and Breckin Meyer. Green will also be writing the movie, and making his directorial debut.

The movie, which will begin filming this week in Thailand, focuses on “Green’s always hesitant character undergoing a personal crisis as he meets up in Thailand with his self-assured best friend, played by Meyer. Others in the movie include Clare Grant, Brenda Song, Macaulay Culkin, Rose Williams and Kedar Williams-Stirling. Deadline adds Orton has a co-starring role in the film.
