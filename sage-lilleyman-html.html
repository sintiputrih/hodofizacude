Sage Lilleyman - Trivia, Family, Bio

Popular YouTuber and internet personality that is most recognized by her self-titled Sage Lilleyman YouTube channel where she posts different cooking videos as well as some DIY and lifestyle videos. She is a huge fan of all things vintage and she loves to portray that in all of her videos. Due to the popularity of her Sage Lilleyman YouTube channel, she has accumulated over 270,000 subscribers and 23 million views.

Before Fame

She first launched her YouTube channel in the year of 2011. Her very first YouTube post was hair tutorial video inspired by Mad Men.

Trivia

She has amassed just over 3.3 million views on one of her most popular YouTube videos. During that September 19, 2020 post, she uploaded a video where she dressed up and acted like a 1950's housewife for a week.

Family Life

She lives along with her fiancé in Doncaster, Australia. 

Associated With

She is a popular YouTube content creator just like Piper Rockelle and Coco Quinn.
