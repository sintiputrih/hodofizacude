Fans Are Getting Major David Hasselhoff Vibes From Frankie Muniz's Recent IG Post

Ah yes, who can remember the hilarious David Hasselhoff eating pizza off the floor video! It went absolutely viral. At that point, his career was on a downward trajectory, as he revealed with Mirror, “Life is funny,” he muses. “I went from box-office gold to box-office poison... this business is so bizarre. Since Knight Rider, I have never had to worry about my next paycheque but I did go out and party too much. I did it for years, but the partying got to be an obsession and wasn’t about having fun anymore. It was about taking me away from being sad.”

Most recently on IG, ‘Malcolm in the Middle’ star Frankie Muniz gave off similar vibes, at least according to fans. It all started when his pie fell on the floor.

Eating Off The Floor

PS... you don't waste @mariecallenders_ mini Apple Pies. They are life.”
