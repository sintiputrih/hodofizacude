Rosala Is a Vixen in a Black Gown and Veil at the 2024 Met Gala

It’s Rosalía’s world and we’re just living in it.

The “Despechá” singer hit the steps of the Metropolitan Museum of Art this evening in a Dior Haute Couture ensemble that radiated true Motomami energy. The ensemble was composed of a jet-black strapless peplum top and a matching long column skirt, which she wore with matching pumps, leather opera gloves, and a lace veil—funeral-chic meets wealthy Italian widow.

She further elevated the glamorous look with a white diamond necklace with a large yellow diamond, plus diamond stud earrings.

As for glam, makeup artist Shelby Smith made the Spanish songstress look like a goddess in natural makeup and a pink lip from Dior Beauty. Her dark chestnut hair was styled in elegant waves.

Kristina Bumphrey//Getty Images

Whether she’s on the red carpet or walking the streets of New York City, Paris, or Los Angeles, Rosalía never leaves a single crumb behind with her fashion choices. This month alone, the singer has already topped our best-dressed lists with her street style.

Related Stories

This past Friday, she arrived for an appearance on the The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in a look that made her look like a luxurious fur ball. The pièce de résistance of the fit was a massive circular coat in ivory fur—dubbed “the Confetti Coat”—from Celine’s Fall/Winter 2024 collection. Under the statement piece, Rosalía wore a micro miniskirt, also from the brand, that was barely visible, as well as sheer black tights and pointy white pumps.

And days earlier, proving that her style cannot be pigeonholed, the singer stepped out in Manhattan in an ensemble that expertly mixed motorcycle style with frilly cottagecore. It consisted of a paper-white flowy dress with ruffles and pleats, plus a cream motorcycle jacket embellished with black studded belts hanging unbuckled from the sides. Rosalía finished the look with a pair of black wrinkly leather boots. Chef’s kiss.
