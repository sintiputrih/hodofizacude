Who Is Ready To Love's DaKiya Lambert? Her Bio, Age, Job, Height

A new batch of Black men and women in their 30s and 40s have come for a new season of Ready to Love on OWN Network. Since Season 5 is already among us, premiered on 28 January 2022, meet one of its contestants, DaKiya Lambert, in this ‘DaKiya Lambert Bio’.

DaKiya Lambert On Ready To Love

This season on Ready to Love, the producers decided to put a bigger emphasis on quality over quantity. So, they took 14 single individuals from Virginia, Washington, D.C., and Maryland. Prior seasons were filled with 20 participants.

So, joining DaKiya Lambert on this journey of finding love are fellow singletons: Kina, Precious, Tiffani, Joi, Ace, and Sabrina as female cast members and Clifton, Demetrius, Laverne, Tory, Fernando, Paul, and Wiley as the male cast members.

Excited about her journey on a reality television show, DaKiya took to her Instagram a day after its first episode — “The journey has officially started! The mansion mixer in Potomac was an experience like none other!!! Nephew Tommy assembled an amazing group of dope men and women to represent the DMV. Do you have any favorites yet? Any early predictions??”.

So far the first episode sees Kina And Fernando eliminated.

Watch the rest of the episodes on Ready To Love on Fridays on the OWN network.

Ready to Love is brought to screens by producer Will Packer and OWN Network which is owned by Oprah Winfrey.

Like old times, the show has been hosted by Thomas “Nephew Tommy” Miles.

DaKiya Lambert Relationship Status Now

DaKiya Lambert appears single as of the time of this writing. That is her reason to be on a reality show hunting for romance.

For her introduction on Ready To Love, DaKiya says she is a sucker for love and that her dating life over the last year and a half has just been swiping right and left. She continues, she is ready to get back out there in the dating game. “Waxed and waxed and ready to meet and mingle and find my man”, said she before going on to her very first date on the show.

How Old Is DaKiya Lambert?

Every year the 2nd of December marks DaKiya Lambert’s birthday making her a Saggitarius. So, on this day in 2021, she turned 40 years old.

So you know, DaKiya shares the same birthday with her sister/cousin Wana. On Facebook, she also mentioned cousin Ari Northington.

DaKiya Lambert Height

Beautiful DaKiya Lambert stands below 5’4” tall.

DaKiya Lambert Job

Having studied at the Dance Institute of Washington, Jones-Haywood School of Ballet, the Atiya Center for Performing Arts, and various other institutions as well as completing a residency with the Dance Theatre of Harlem, DaKiya Lambert began her professional dance career as a member of Xpressions Contemporary Dance Company.

With the company, DaKiya was allowed to work with artists from prestigious companies such as Alvin Ailey American Dance Theatre, Dance Theatre of Harlem, Philadanco, Dallas Black Dance Theatre, and Dayton Contemporary Dance Company.​ Upon returning to Washington, D.C, she began teaching and choreographing for various groups, churches, and dance schools in the area.

Then, in 2005, DaKiya became the Director of Premiere Teen Dance Company. Two years later she fulfilled her lifelong dream of opening a dance studio: Dimensions. Since its inception, Dimensions has grown into a most sought-after school for the area’s most talented dancers.

Find the dance studio on Facebook @DanceDimensionsMD and Twitter @Dimensions_Inc. Based in Forestville, Maryland, they have been offering classes from ages 3-adult in ballet, pointe, Horton, jazz, tap, contemporary, Dunham, hip hop, and Zumba.

Has DaKiya Lambert Revealed Her Family?

DaKiya Lambert’s mom is Kathy Green McDaniel (who is on Instagram @kit_kat64).

Although DaKiya also mentioned two other mothers in an Instagram post on Mother’s Day writing “Happy Mother’s Day to my 3 moms!!! It takes a village to raise a child and these 3 women were the foundations of my village.”

Then, there is DaKiya’s little brother Rodney (on Instagram @itsrodzilla). According to his Facebook, he is a former NBA dancer and graphic designer, who studied at Towson University, a public university in Towson, Maryland. He is currently based in District Heights, Maryland.
