Complete Spoiler Lineup For WWE Elimination Chamber

WWE’s Elimination Chamber will be a huge night in Montreal. They will very likely sell out the arena completely, and now we have a lineup so fans know what to expect.

Click here for more spoilers on WWE’s plan for the actual Elimination Chamber matches tonight.

Sean Sapp reported tonight’s planned lineup for Elimination Chamber. Obviously, Roman Reigns vs Sami Zayn will headline the show, and the other two Elimination Chamber matches will also hold prominent spots on the card.

– Women’s Elimination Chamber match: Liv Morgan vs. Asuka vs. Raquel Rodriguez vs. Natalya vs. Carmella vs. Nikki Cross

– Brock Lesnar vs. Bobby Lashley

– Edge & Beth Phoenix vs. Finn Balor & Rhea Ripley

– Men’s Elimination Chamber: Seth Rollins vs. Montez Ford vs. Johnny Gargano vs. Austin Theory vs. Damian Priest vs. Bronson Reed.

– Sami Zayn vs. Roman Reigns

It was also noted that, “The main event is slated for 40 minutes, though we aren’t sure if that includes entrances or post-match. This should be noted for ALL matches.”

We will have to see how it all pans out when the show goes live. Elimination Chamber is always an exciting event, because you never know what can happen on the road to WrestleMania.
