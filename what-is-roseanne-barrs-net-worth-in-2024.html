What is Roseanne Barr's Net Worth in 2024

Roseanne Barr, an American comedian, actress, writer, television producer, and director, has made a significant impact in the entertainment industry.

Her career, spanning several decades, has not only brought her fame but also substantial wealth. In 2024, her net worth stands at an estimated $80 million, making her one of the world's richest female comedians.

Early Life and Career

Born Roseanne Cherrie Barr on November 3, 1952, in Salt Lake City, Utah, Roseanne began her career in stand-up comedy before venturing into television.

She gained widespread recognition for her self-titled sitcom "Roseanne," which aired from 1988 to 1997, garnering numerous accolades and awards, including an Emmy and a Golden Globe.

Source of Wealth

Roseanne's net worth of $80 million primarily stems from various sources, including:

  • Books
  • Stand-up Comedy
  • Television

Earnings & Financial Data

Television Salary

During the last season of her sitcom, Roseanne earned an impressive $21 million in salary.

Real Estate Investments

Throughout her career, Roseanne has made significant real estate investments, including:

  • A 28,000-square-foot mansion in southeast Iowa, which was later donated to a foundation
  • A 10,000 square foot home in Rolling Hills, California, which she sold for a substantial profit
  • Multiple properties in California, including homes in El Segundo and Playa Del Rey
  • A 2,212-square-foot ranch-style house and a 46-acre property in Hawaii

Personal Life

Roseanne's personal life has also contributed to her public image and financial history. She has been married multiple times and has five children.

Notable events in her personal life include her widely criticized rendition of "The Star-Spangled Banner" and her presidential run in 2012 as part of the Green Party.

Comparison with Other Female Comedians

In a report by CEOWORLD magazine, Roseanne Barr ranks as the fifth richest female comedian in the world, with a net worth of $80 million.

This places her among other prominent female comedians such as Ellen DeGeneres, Julia Louis-Dreyfus, Rosie O’Donnell, and Melissa McCarthy.

Recent Controversies

Roseanne has been involved in several controversies throughout her career, including posing as Adolf Hitler in a feature for a satirical Jewish publication and making controversial statements on social media.

These incidents have not only impacted her public image but may also have affected her financial standing.

 

Ultimately, Roseanne Barr's journey in the entertainment industry has been marked by successes, controversies, and significant financial achievements. With a net worth of $80 million in 2024, she continues to be a prominent figure in the world of comedy and entertainment.

Her net worth in 2024 reflects the culmination of her multifaceted career, impactful real estate investments, and a prominent position among the world's richest female comedians.
