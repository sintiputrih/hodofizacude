Junaid Safdar is famous as the grandson of 3-time former Pakistani Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif. Junaid Safdar’s grandfather is also the current vice president of the Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz. His mother is also a famous political figure.

Early Life

Junaid Safdar was born on 1 May 1996 and he is currently 27 years old. He hails from Lahore, Pakistan, and his zodiac sign is Taurus.

Talking about his family, his father is Captain (retd) Muhammad Safdar Awan and his mother is Maryam Sharif.

Likewise, his maternal grandfather is Nawaz Sharif and his maternal grandmother is Kulsoom Nawaz. His maternal grandfather is a three-time former Prime Minister of Pakistan.

Similarly, his paternal grandfather is Muhammad Ishaq.

Regarding his educational journey, Safdar attended University College London (UCL) where he obtained his BA degree in Political Science from the Department of Science.

Likewise, he graduated in International Relations from Durham University, School of International Relations.

Also, he completed his M.Sc. in Governance and Ethics from UCL’s Department of Governance and Ethics. Before all this, he completed his junior college with an ‘O’ grade from Aitchison College, Lahore.

Moreover, his full name is Muhammad Junaid Safdar, and is more popular by his middle name Junaid.

Career

Professionally, Junaid is a young politician. He is associated with the Pakistani Muslim League (PML). When attending college in London, the London Police arrested this personality.

The arrest was due to Safdar’s verbal spat with the supporters of PTI. The PTI supporters initially it by calling him names due to which Safdar became angry and continued the verbal fight.

The arrest was done on a Thursday night outside the Avenfield apartments. The London Police released him as well as Zakari Sharif without charge.

Zakari is the son of Hussain Nawaz and Safdar’s cousin. However, this incident wasn’t a good sign for the PML party as his parents – Maryam Nawaz and Muhammad Safdar Awan, and grandfather Nawaz Sharif were all detained at Adiala Jail.

To talk in detail about the incident that led to this young guy’s arrest, it happened when both Safdar and Sharif were involved in a fight with approximately 7 Pakistani Tehreek-e-Insaf protestors.

These protesters were present at the doorstep of Avenfield flats and hurled abuses at the two as they made their way inside their flats. As a result, Safdar acted in self-defense when attacked.

Besides this physical altercation in 2018, this personality has made little headlines. He has maintained a good private life away from the public eye.

The Prime Minister of Pakistan – Imran Khan – briefly mentioned this personality in a speech regarding a polo match that he partook in. His grandfather had attended this match as well.
