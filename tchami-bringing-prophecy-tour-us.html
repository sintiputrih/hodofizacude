Tchami Announces North American Prophecy Tour For 2017

Recommended Videos

Tchami is gearing up for a major 2017 with a new announcement today. The future house mainstay has kept our attention throughout the year with his first ever essential mix, as well as a major pair up with Malaa on “Prophecy,” followed by an equally huge remix EP that just came out. Now, the French producer intends to keep the ball rolling in 2017 as he announces the upcoming Prophecy Tour.

Named after his collaboration with Malaa, Tchami’s Prophecy Tour will feature support from Mercer and is set to kick off on February 2, 2017 in Orlando. The tour will feature stops throughout North America, touching down in cities like Atlanta, Washington DC, Toronto, Denver and LA to name a few, with more dates to be announced in the future.

Tchami first introduced us to his cathedral stage production on his last tour, and the stunning presentation is set to resurface on the Prophecy Tour. Select dates will also feature the backing choir that captivated listeners at Coachella earlier in the year.
