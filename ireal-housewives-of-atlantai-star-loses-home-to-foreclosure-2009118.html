Real Housewives of Atlanta Star Loses Home to Foreclosure

Real Housewives of Atlanta star Lisa Wu-Hartwell lost her Georgia home to foreclosure, TMZ.com reports.

Take a look at other reality stars with bad luck.

Hartwell and her husband, former Oakland Raiders linebacker Edgerton Hartwell, were evicted from their multi-million-dollar mansion last week after the couple reportedly defaulted on their loan. The bank sold it shortly after.
