Cardi B set to release sexy athleisure and a matching sneaker with Reebok

Hip-hop sensation Cardi B has teamed up yet again with Reebok on an all-new apparel and footwear capsule.

The ‘Let Me Be…In My World’ collection will officially drop on August 27, alongside a new Cardi B Classic Leather sneaker.

‘What inspired the designs of this collection? Uh, snatchness,’ said the Grammy award winner. 

The sexy line of athleisure includes corset tops, crop jackets, tracksuits and tight leggings that match perfectly with the collection’s sneaker offerings.
