What Does Neil Patrick Harris Find Interesting? Find Out In His New Newsletter - Wondercade!

Neil Patrick Harris just launched Wondercade, a free, weekly email newsletter that has been inspired by all of his interest and passions – food and drink, design and decor, experiences and games!

“I’ve always loved entertaining, and I seek out new experiences whenever and wherever I can. While I do enjoy sharing my passions with the world on various social media platforms, I’m supremely thrilled to now have a singular dedicated destination to celebrate the people, places, and things I find interesting,” said NPH. “Wondercade’s mission statement is that ‘life should be entertaining’. My hope is that, with Wondercade, we all can eat and drink more tastefully, travel and consume more adventurously, live more stylishly and just generally be reminded that a life spent in good company is a life well-lived.”

Be sure to sign up at Wondercade.com and be on the look out for the debut newsletter on Wednesday, October 6th and every Wednesday afternoon thereafter!

NPH recently made headlines for not talking to actress Mayim Bialik for a very long time.
