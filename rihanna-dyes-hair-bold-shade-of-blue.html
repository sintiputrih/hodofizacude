Rihanna debuts new edgy blue hair during concert

Jaws dropped to the floor as Rihanna took to the stage for a recent concert, and it wasn't just because of the singer's amazing vocals. The 27-year-old flaunted her edgy beauty credentials as she debuted a striking new bright blue hair colour.

Rihanna has dyed her hair a bold shade of blue

The Umbrella hitmaker was dazzling as she performed at CBS Radio's third annual We Can Survive concert, wearing her glossy curly tresses up in a high ponytail that showed off her bold new look.

Click for more celebrities with bright coloured hair

Her outfit was equally eye-catching, with the star opting for an oversized pinstripe blazer and trouser combo, paired with a lace crop top giving fans a glimpse of her enviably toned figure. Showing off her high fashion personal style, Rihanna completed her ensemble with a pair of retro round glasses and a sparkling statement black and silver ring.

Earlier this month, the singer opened up about the relationship she experienced with ex-boyfriend Chris Brown, admitting she still cares about the rapper.

Rihanna debuted her new look during a concert

"I was very protective of him," Rihanna told Vanity Fair. "I felt that people didn't understand him. Even after." She added: "I don't hate him. I will care about him until the day I die."

As to her current love life, the Diamonds singer admitted she doesn't have time for a relationship at the moment due to her work commitments.

"I have so much work to do that I get distracted," said the artist. "I don't have time to be lonely. And I get fearful of relationships because I feel guilty about wanting someone to be completely faithful and loyal, when I can't even give them ten per cent of the attention that they need. It's just the reality of my time, my life, my schedule."
