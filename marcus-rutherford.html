Marcus Rutherford Height, Weight, Age, Biography, Girlfriend &amp; Family

Marcus Rutherford (born in 1995) is a British Ambitious Actor, Model and Public Figure from Nottingham, England. He is one of those artists who gained fame and success after the release of his debut movie.

On November 07, 2018, Marcus’ first drama project called “OBEY” released on the silver screen. He portrayed the lead role of Leon who struggles with his horrible past due to mother’s alcohol addiction.

Later, Leon met a girl named Twiggy played by Sophie Kennedy Clark and they fell in love with each other. The story revolves around the main hero and what happens when Twiggy rejects him to carry forward their affair.
