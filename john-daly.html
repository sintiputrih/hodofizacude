Who Is John Daly? Know His Wives, Controversies &amp; Net Worth

John Daly is a professional golfer associated with the PGA Tour. He goes by the name Long John. Outside golf, he has also been in the news for his previous marriages, particularly Bettye Fulford. The other woman he has been married to are Sherrie Miller, Paulette Dean Daly, and Dale Crafton.

Daly has an estimated net worth of $2 Million. His zodiac sign is Taurus and he is also the father of three kids named John Patrick Daly II, Shynah Hale Daly, and Sierra Lynn Daly. Let's learn about the controversial golf figure.

John Daly at one point had lost all hope in love because of his four failed marriages. His four ex-spouses are Sherrie Miller, Bettye Fulford, Paulette Dean Daly, and Dale Crafton.

A Brief On Daly's Golfing Career

John is known for mostly headlines media and tabloids for a lot of wrong reasons. But, that doesn't take away the fact that he is still a top athlete. He is regarded as the OG bad boy in golf as he broke the gentlemen's image of what a golfer should be like.

Other than his personality, Daly has also managed to achieve a lot on the golf course. He has won 19 tournaments so far in his career, including 5 in PGA Tours, 3 in European Tour, 2 in Sunshine Tour, 1 in the Asian and Ben Hogan Tour.  

Long John's official website also says that he became a professional in 1987 and had his first win in the same year in Missouri Open. The website also says that he won his first PGA championship in the debut PGA Tour in 1991 where he was also honored with the Rookie of the Year award.

Career As A Singer

If you think golf is Daly's only passion then you are wrong, as the man is a singer too. He is pretty serious about singing as he has already released two albums, I Only Know One Way and Whiskey & Water. 

The controversial golfer has released a lot of hit songs like Hit It Hard, Cold Beer, and Blue Collar Golfer. His song Hit It Hard is his most streamed song on Spotify with over 1.9 Million streams.

Why Did The PGA Tour Suspend John Daly In The Past?

The five tournaments winner in the PGA Tour, Daly found landed himself in hot water that saw him getting banned from the tour for six months. He was banned in December 2008 and it was multiple reasons that led the PGA to make this decision.

Essentially Sports has mentioned all the reasons behind Daly's suspension. He was found to be spending some time with the hooter's ladies at Innisbrook. Other incidents involve the Pro-Am Tournament and the time he got drunk which led to the arrest. 

Currently Battling With Cancer

Daly is a cancer patient as he is suffering from bladder cancer. He was first diagnosed with it in September 2020. Luckily, it was in its initial stage meaning he can recover from it. He is going through treatment at the time of this writing.

The golfer had to give up on a lot of things as a result of it. He has revealed that he had to give up on his favorite things like beef, milk, alcohol, and cigarettes as a result of this.
