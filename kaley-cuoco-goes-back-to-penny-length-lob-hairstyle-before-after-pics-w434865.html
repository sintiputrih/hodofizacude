Kaley Cuoco Goes Back to 'Penny'-Length Lob Hairstyle: Before, After Pics

A little snip, snip! Kaley Cuoco got back into character for season 10 of The Big Bang Theory by debuting a major hair makeover on Friday, August 12.

Cuoco, 25, who is currently dating equestrian Karl Cook after splitting from husband Ryan Sweeting in September 2015, chopped her chest-level tresses into a collarbone-grazing lob for the show. “Sending mad 💗 and props to @faye.woods for bringing my cut and colour back to its ‘Penny’ roots #nofilter,” the 8 Simple Rules alum wrote, calling out the show’s hairstylist.

Her new ‘do is about more than just length, however. It takes her from a dark-rooted ombre style back to the bright, lived-in blonde of her Big Bang Theory character, Penny.

null

The actress is clearly excited to see her castmates again, posting multiple ‘grams in the studio with her pals. On August 11, Cuoco shared a picture of herself and costar (and former beau) Johnny Galecki on set, writing, “@sanctionedjohnnygalecki we back!! @thebigbangtheory__ #season10.”

Cuoco followed it up a week later by enthusiastically sharing an snap of the rest of the cast in full costume. “#season10 woot woot!” she wrote on Tuesday, August 16. “@thebigbangtheory__ we back babyyyyy😎🙌🏽🙏🏼 #selfiestick #backstagepass.”
