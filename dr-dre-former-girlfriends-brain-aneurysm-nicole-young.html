Who are Dr Dre's ex-girlfriends?

DR Dre was rushed to a Los Angeles hospital on January 5, after suffering a "brain aneurysm."

Dre, 55, has had his share of lovers throughout his life, and is currently battling his ex-wife Nicole Young in their nasty divorce.

Who are Dr Dre's ex-girlfriends?

Cassandra Joy Greene (1981-1983)

In December 1981, Dre, who was 16 at the time, began dating Cassandra Joy Greene, 15.

The two had a son together, Curtis, who was raised by Greene.

When Curtis was 16, Dre finally recognized him as his son, and met him 20 years after he was born.

Curtis is a rapper under the name Hood Surgeon.

Clara Moroni (1982-1984)

In 1982, Dre began dating Italian music artist Clara Moroni.

The two had a daughter together, La Tanya Danielle, in 1983.

Dre and Moroni called it quits in 1984.

Jenita Porter (1987-1988)

In 1997, Dre began dating Jenita Porter, and had a son together Andre Young Jr.

In August 2008, Dre's son Andre died at the age of 20 from an overdose of heroin and morphine at his mother's home in Woodland Hills, California.

Michel'le (1988-1996)

Dre began dating singer Michel'le in 1988, who frequently contributed vocals for Ruthless Records and Death Row Records albums.

Michel'le is an American R&B singer, songwriter and rapper.

Her career peaked in the late 1980s and early 1990s, with songs like No More Lies and Something in My Heart.

In 1991, Dre and Michel'le had a son together, Marcel.

Michel'le left Dre in 1996 after she discovered that he was engaged to another woman.

In the years following their separation, Michel'le claimed Dre was physically abusive to her throughout their relationship.

In an interview with The Breakfast Club, she recalled her injuries included a broken nose, a cracked rib and five black eyes.

She also claimed that after one argument Dre shot at her, missing her by a few inches.

Nicole Young (1996-2020)

In 1995, Dre began seeing Nicole Young, and got married after just four months of dating.

The couple have two children together Truice and their daughter Truly.

The couple revealed they were getting divorced in June 2020, with Young citing irreconcilable differences as the reason behind the couple's split.

This will be Young's second divorce as she was formerly married to NBA player Sedale Threatt.

The last time Dr Dre and his soon-to-be-ex-wife were seen together was February 2020 at a Tom Ford fashion show.

In September 2020 it was reported that Nicole was seeking $2million a month in spousal support,

But in October a judge ruled in favor of the rapper not forking out $1.5million in security fees for his ex wife.

She also accused him of hiding valuable assets after they split - including trademarks for his stage name and "The Chronic" album, according to TMZ.

Sources close to Dre told the outlet that her claim is "ridiculous" and is an attempt to embarrass him.

The sources told TMZ: "They were married in 1996. Chronic came out in '92 and Dr. Dre used his name since the 80s, so it's all his and his alone."

She responded by calling Dre's move an "epic failure and reveals the true nature of his character, or lack thereof."

Before her marriage to Dre, she worked as an attorney.
