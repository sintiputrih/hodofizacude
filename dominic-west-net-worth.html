Dominic Gerard Fe West (born (1969-10-15)15 October 1969) is an English actor. He is best known for his role as Detective Jimmy McNulty in the HBO drama series The Wire. West won the award for Leading Actor at the 2012 British Academy Television Awards for his role as serial killer Fred West in Appropriate Adult. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿

#Fact
1Dominic West portrayed late author George MacDonald Fraser's Victorian anti-hero Harry Flashman, the main character of Fraser's "Flashman" novels, at a 2014 auction of Fraser's library. West also stated at the time that he would like to see a new Flashman movie made for the new generation. The only previous movie, Royal Flash (1975) was not a success and was so disliked by Fraser that he would not allow another one to be made in his lifetime.
2He is a first cousin, once removed, of American politician Thomas Eagleton, who was briefly the 1972 Democratic nominee for Vice President of the United States. Dominic's paternal great-grandparents, Thomas Francis Eagleton and Mary Theresa Hennelly (who had moved to Missouri from Ireland), were also the paternal grandparents of politician Eagleton.
3Appearing as 'Jan' in the play "Rock & Roll" at the Duke of York Theatre in London. [February 2007]
4Has played ancient world warriors twice with Michael Fassbender: 300 (2006) and Centurion (2010).
5He has two daughters; his eldest, Martha West and Dora; and two sons, Senan and Francis with his wife Catherine Fitzgerald.
6Dominic West's maternal grandparents were Irish and his paternal grandparents were English.and Irish.
