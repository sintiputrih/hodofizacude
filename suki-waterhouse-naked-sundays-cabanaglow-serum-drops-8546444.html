Suki Waterhouse Enhanced Her Pregnancy Glow With Highlighting Serum Drops

I don’t know when exactly the shift in pregnancy fashion happened, but I’m glad it did. For way too long, pregnant women were expected to wear nothing but drab, mumu-like silhouettes. But as evidenced by Rihanna and, more recently, Suki Waterhouse, fashion can and will adjust. Waterhouse looked fabulous at last week’s Emmys in a vibrant red gown, and her skin was absolutely radiant. It was definitely in part due to her natural pregnancy glow, but boosted by Naked Sundays’ new CabanaGlow SPF 50 Mineral Glow Serum Drops. 

The CabanaGlow Drops are a serum-makeup hybrid, similar to Drunk Elephant’s viral bronzing iteration. They racked up a 100,000-plus pre-launch waitlist and are available in three shades: bronze, illuminating (light gold), and pearlescent (a pearly white). According to the brand, Waterhouse’s makeup artist Benjamin Puckey said, “I used the CabanaGlow SPF50 Mineral Glow Serum Drops in the shade illuminating… as a face and body highlighter that gives your skin that lit-from-within glow.” These drops are multi-use, so you can make like Puckey and apply to both face and body as a highlighter, or use as an under-paint, primer, glowy skin tint, etc. 

If this is your first exposure to Naked Sundays, it’s an Australian sun protection and SPF-infused makeup brand — important to note because Australia has some of the strictest SPF regulations. Your Naked Sundays suncare products will likely be the most effective you’ve ever used. 

Depending on the shade, the CabanaGlow Drops are a pigmented highlighter, glow-enhancer, or bronzer. They can also be used as serums thanks to an ingredient lineup of pink algae, hyaluronic acid, and rosella extract. Algae is the superstar here — according to a study it’s antioxidant-rich and combats inflammation, hyperpigmentation, and signs of aging like wrinkles. Hyaluronic acid is a humectant that plumps skin by preventing moisture loss and reduces the appearance of lines and wrinkles. Lastly, rosella extract is an additional boost of antioxidant and dark spot-reducing prowess.  

Head to Naked Sundays to shop the glow-enhancing CabanaGlow Serum Drops in bronze, illuminating, and pearlescent.
